Mr T's is a family ran fast food restaurant, opening in 2016. The menu consists of burgers & fries, pizza, chicken wings, parmesans and shakes, giving it an American feel. Along with this, there are artworks on the walls of iconic sports stars and musicians. Mains prices range from £16.29 to £6.89. My standout recommendation from the menu would have to be their box meal, with a burger, fries, chicken wings, stir fried donner and a drink of your choice all for £12.99.
