food

Some of the best restaurants, dessert parlors and takeaway spots on the Curry Mile.

Bardez is a relatively new restaurant serving delicious Idian cuisine, opening in 2023. Their menu has a range of options, including vegitarian options. The staff are extremely friendly, making sure each customer feels like they are special- even shaking hands as diners exit. Despite the high class feel of the restaurant, the food is reasonably priced, with mains ranging from £7.95 to £17.95. My top picks from the menu would have to be the mango lassi and the Lamb Shank Biryani. no.35

The Rusholme Chippy is a "no frills" cafe serving a mix of both Middle Eastern and British cuisine, established in 1977. Their clay oven cuisine secret recipes have kept Mancunians coming back to the shop for decades. The cafe has a very friendly vibe, and staff make sure you feel welcome. When I was in there the manager even took the time to explain a little bit of the shop's history to me while I enjoyed one of their Single Kobeda Kebabs. The Rusholme Chippy is undoubtably a land mark of the Curry Mile, and is definitely worth a visit. For updates and more information, check out their facebook page.

Mr T's is a family ran fast food restaurant, opening in 2016. The Mr T's menu consists of burgers & fries, pizza, chicken wings, parmesans and shakes, giving it an American feel. Along with this, there are artworks on the walls of iconic sports stars and musicians. Mains prices range from £16.29 to £6.89. My standout recommendation from the menu would have to be their box meal, with a burger, fries, chicken wings, stir fried donner and a drink of your choice all for £12.99.

If you've got a sweet tooth make sure you check out Sanam Sweethouse and Restaurant, stocking a range of beautifully decorated cakes and a miryad of confectionary. Check out the Sanam website for the menu and more information.
