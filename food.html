MR T's

Mr T's is a family ran fast food restaurant, opening in 2016. The menu consists of burgers & fries, pizza, chicken wings, parmesans and shakes, giving it an American feel. Along with this, there are artworks on the walls of Biggie Smalls and Tupac on the walls wearing neon light crowns. Mains prices range from £16.29 to £6.89.
