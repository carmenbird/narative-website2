Wilmslow Road, also known as The Curry Mile, is famous for it's food and culture. The road started to develop into the 'Curry Mile' in the 1950s, when South Asian migrants came into Rusholme and the surrounding areas to fill British labour shortages. Over time more and more cuisine shops popped up along the strip, and by the mid 80s the the title of 'The Curry Mile' had become commonplace.
