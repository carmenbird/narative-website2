Some of the different ways to be entertained on Wilmslow road.

After enjoying the different cuisine options on the road take a relaxing walk through the Whitworth Gallery. Established in 1889 the gallery was founded in memory of industrialist Sir Joseph Whitworth as a part of The University Of Manchester. Almost all the exhibitions are free, along with various events held in the space. The art in the gallery ranges from historic to contempary, there's something for everyone. For more information check out the info page on the Uni Of Manchester website.

A walk through Whitworth Park is a great way to clear your mind, while getting away from the busyness of inner city Manchester. With it's playground, nature, and artistic statues, Witworth park has something for the whole family.
